Help

Email notification trigger sends an email to the defined receiver when the trigger executes. To set up the trigger settings, aside from setting up the basic configuration (common for all types of the triggers, it is necessary to configure additional settings:
  • SMTP Server name SMTP server IP address.
  • Port SSL Port supported SSL protocol.
  • Account name Your login to the email account.
  • Password Password to the email account.
  • Sender email Your full email address.
  • Receiver email Email address to which the email will be sent.
  • Title Title of the email.
  • Content Content of the message.
